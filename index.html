I am a senior majoring in Linguistics at The Ohio State University. My research interests include experimental semantics and pragmatics, as well as computational linguistics.

I am interested in how people use and interpret meaning in communication, especially implied meaning, pragmatic inference, and the interaction between linguistic theory and experimental evidence.

I also use computational methods to study language, and I am interested in how large language models can help us understand meaning, inference, and language more broadly.
